# Runner's Pace | Fridge Combo #79

A high-precision, zero-friction pace calculator designed for athletes, marathoners, and track runners. Get your split times and average speed instantly without annoying ads or complex menus.

## Core Philosophy
- **1 Tool / 1 Action**: Enter your distance and target time to see your pace immediately.
- **Athlete-First UI**: High-contrast display optimized for use at the track or gym, even with shaky hands after a hard run.
- **Zero-Waste Workflow**: Smart time parsing (e.g., type "2200" for 22:00) and one-tap presets for standard race distances.

## Key Features
- **Smart Time Parsing**: Enter time as a continuous string of numbers. The tool intelligently maps them (e.g., "2200" becomes 22:00, "10500" becomes 1:05:00).
- **Kilometer & Mile Toggle**: Switch between metric and imperial units with a single tap. Your entered distance converts automatically.
- **Track Lap (400m) Split**: Automatically calculates your 400m lap time—essential for interval training on the track.
- **Quick Distance Presets**: One-tap buttons for 5K, 10K, Half, and Full Marathons.

## How to Use
1. **Choose Unit**: Select Kilometers or Miles.
2. **Set Distance**: Tap a preset button (5K/10K/Half/Full) or enter a custom distance.
3. **Enter Time**: Type your goal time in the HHMMSS format (e.g., "2200" for a 22-minute 5K).
4. **Read Results**: Instantly view your required Pace, 400m Lap time, and Average Speed.

## Design System
- **Background**: #fff
- **Main Text**: #333
- **Borders**: #ccc (High contrast for visibility)
- **Border Radius**: 15px (All elements)
- **Mobile Optimized**: Double-tap zoom prevention on buttons and numeric-ready input fields.

## Privacy & Policy
- **No Login**
- **No Tracking**
- **No Ads**
- **Browser Only**: Your data is never uploaded; calculations happen locally.
